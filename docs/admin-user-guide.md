# Salon Management System - Admin/User Guide

## 1. Login and Roles
- Owner: full access.
- Manager: operations + reports.
- Staff: appointments, attendance, customer operations, loyalty actions.

## 2. Core Modules
- `Appointments`: create, edit, transition status, cancel.
- `Services`: manage service catalog (duration, buffer, price, repeat rules).
- `Staff`: profiles and activation.
- `Schedules`: shift and break setup.
- `Attendance`: clock in/out and late tracking.
- `Leave Requests`: submit and approve/reject.

## 3. Customer and CRM
- `Customers`: maintain profile, allergies, birthday, source, notes.
- `CRM Automation`:
  - tags and assignments,
  - segment rules (create/edit/deactivate/preview/run),
  - due-service generation/reminders,
  - campaign templates and campaigns (manual and scheduled dispatch).

## 4. Loyalty
- Configure `Auto Earn Rules` in Loyalty screen.
- Manage tiers, multipliers, rewards.
- Redeem points and apply manual adjustments.
- Award configured bonuses (`referral`, `review`, `birthday`).

## 5. Inventory and Procurement
- `Inventory`: products, stock adjustments, low-stock alerts.
- `Suppliers`: supplier master records.
- `Purchase Orders`: create, edit draft, approve, receive, cancel.

## 6. Reports
- Filter by date range.
- Review KPI cards and charts.
- Export CSV and summary PDF.

## 7. Scheduler Jobs
- Due-service reminders: daily at 10:00.
- Scheduled campaign dispatch: every 10 minutes.
